Press ` to start/stop the timers.

  • First press: starts Player 1/Survivor timer
  • Second press: stops Survivor timer and swaps player positions
  • Third press: starts Survivor timer
  • Fourth press: stops Survivor timer and awards point to the player with the longest time
  • Fifth press: resets the timers to 0:00.00
 In the event of a tie, no points are awarded.
